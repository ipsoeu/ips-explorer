Service: Digital credentials for graduates

Responsible organisation: University of Lille (Academic-Research)

Since July, 2021, the University of Lille issues digital credentials for all its graduates, using blockchain technology. University students receive a link to display a digital credential after their graduation being able to store it in a secure personal wallet and present it to any other university, administration or recruitment platform, ensuring that the credentials are instantly recognized as valid and authentic, without having to contact the issuing university.
